Sure! The 'r' sound is made with your tongue. Here's a simple exercise to help you:

  • Say the letter 'r' slowly, and make sure your tongue is touching the roof of your mouth.
  • Practice saying words that contain the 'r' sound, like "red," "rain," and "read."
